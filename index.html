Maddie & Billy Are Stuck with Each Other

When Is It?

Maddie and Billy are getting married on Friday, August 7, 2015 at Prairie Production, a loft space in Chicago located at 1314 W. Randolph.

The ceremony will begin at 5:30PM in the courtyard, followed by cocktails and hors-d’oeuvre, then dinner and dancing inside. Feel free to arrive early and grab a drink, because TGIF.

How Do I Get There?

Limited metered and non-metered street parking is available in the neighborhood, but because we’re getting married during rush hour on a Friday in Chicago, we heartily encourage our guests to take advantage of the city's robust public transit system, myriad taxi cabs, or ride-sharing service of their choice.

Amtrak

Guests coming from Milwaukee can take the Hiawatha Amtrak train to Union Station, which is 1.5 miles away from the venue. Taxi cabs are readily available at the station’s Canal Street exit.

Metra

Guests coming from Kenosha can take the Union Pacific North Line train to Ogilvie Station, which is 1.2 miles away from the venue. Taxi cabs are readily available at street level.

CTA

The venue is 0.5 miles away from the Ashland/Lake Green line stop. Nearby buses include the #9 Ashland and #20 Madison.

Ride Sharing

Uber, Lyft and Sidecar apps are all available in Chicago.
